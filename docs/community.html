Community

OpenRA is a game created by the community, for the community. You can contribute in a variety of ways, from giving help to the dev team, to finding and reporting bugs, to just playing the game!

Join the Discussion
If you've got questions or you just feel like chatting, come on down to our discord or #openra on Freenode.

We've even got an old fashioned forum full of conversations about maps, releases, balance, and off topic chatter!

Create or Download Maps for OpenRA
No real time strategy game would be complete without its maps. OpenRA has its own resource website where you can find over 9,000 maps to add all sorts of variety to the game. You can even create and upload your own maps for other people to play!
